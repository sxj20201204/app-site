Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

SnapBatch is an offline photo export utility. This Privacy Policy explains how SnapBatch handles information when you use the app.

1. Summary

SnapBatch is designed to process your selected images on your device. We do not require an account, and we do not operate a backend for image processing.

2. Information We Access

SnapBatch may access the following information only when needed for app functionality:

  • Photos you explicitly choose from your device photo library
  • Files you choose to export or share
  • Basic app preferences stored locally on your device

3. How Your Data Is Used

SnapBatch uses selected content only to provide app features such as:

  • Batch resizing images
  • Compressing files
  • Converting export format
  • Renaming exported files
  • Saving or sharing exported results

4. No Cloud Processing

SnapBatch does not upload your images to our servers for processing. Image processing is intended to happen locally on your device.

5. No Account Required

SnapBatch does not require account creation, login, or profile registration to use the core app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your selected photos with third parties through a SnapBatch backend because SnapBatch does not rely on a backend for image processing.

7. Local Storage

SnapBatch may store app preferences locally on your device, such as display settings and export defaults. Exported files may be saved to your Photos library, Files app, or shared through destinations you choose.

8. Third-Party Services

If you use Apple system features such as Photos, Files, or Share Sheet, those features are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

9. Data Retention

Because SnapBatch is designed as an offline utility, selected photos are processed for the requested export task. App preferences may remain on your device until you reset the app, remove the app, or change those preferences.

10. Children's Privacy

SnapBatch is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a SnapBatch backend service.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@snapbatch.app
