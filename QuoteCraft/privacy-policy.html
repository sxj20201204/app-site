QuoteCraft

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QuoteCraft handles information when you use the app.

1. Summary

QuoteCraft is designed as an offline-first app. The app does not require account creation, does not operate a backend for user data, and does not intentionally collect personal data from users through remote servers.

2. Information You Enter in the App

You may enter information such as project names, client names, pricing details, business details, notes, and exported quote content. This information is stored locally on your device so the app can function.

3. Data Storage

QuoteCraft stores app data locally on your device. This may include:

  • Saved quotes
  • Saved templates
  • App settings such as display name, business name, email, currency, and default pricing preferences
  • Generated PDF exports that you choose to create

QuoteCraft does not provide cloud sync or account-based storage in the current version.

4. Data Sharing

QuoteCraft does not automatically share your data with the developer or third parties.

If you choose to export a PDF or share content using iOS system share features, that action is initiated by you and handled through Apple system services or the destination you select.

5. Analytics and Tracking

QuoteCraft does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not use remote analytics services in the current version.

6. Internet Connectivity

QuoteCraft is intended to work without a backend. If future versions introduce optional online functionality, this Privacy Policy may be updated before those changes are made available.

7. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it, delete the app, or remove exported files yourself.

8. Children’s Privacy

QuoteCraft is not directed to children under the age of 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Choices

You control the information you enter into QuoteCraft. You may edit or delete data inside the app at any time. You may also remove the app from your device to delete app data stored locally, subject to iOS device behavior and backups under your control.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If material changes are made, the updated version will be published with a revised effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at support@QuoteCraft.app.
