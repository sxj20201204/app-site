Electrician Toolkit

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Electrician Toolkit ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App.

1. Summary

Electrician Toolkit is designed to work primarily offline. We do not require account registration and we do not operate a backend service for storing your calculation data or presets.

2. Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect personal information through the App.

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved presets
  • Recent calculator usage
  • Favorite calculators
  • App preferences required for normal operation

3. How Your Data Is Used

Local device data is used only to provide App functionality, including:

  • Restoring your saved presets
  • Displaying recent and favorite tools
  • Improving convenience and workflow inside the App

4. Data Storage

Data created in the App is stored locally on your device using Apple platform storage mechanisms. We do not upload this data to our servers because the App currently does not provide user accounts or cloud syncing operated by us.

5. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

The App is not intended to track you across apps or websites. The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs and is not designed to build advertising profiles about users.

6. Third-Party Services

If you download the App through the Apple App Store, Apple may process information in accordance with its own privacy practices. That processing is governed by Apple's policies, not this Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Because the App does not operate a backend account system, we generally do not receive the local data you create in the App.

8. Data Retention

Data stored by the App remains on your device until you delete it, remove the App, or reset your device backups and storage according to your own device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We rely on Apple platform security features and local device protections. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Use

The App may be made available globally. Because core App data is stored locally on your device, your use of the App may be subject to the privacy and consumer laws that apply in your jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at the page where this policy is made available, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@electriciantoolkit.app
